Animal Crossing is a game series that's been around since 2001. The latest incarnation, Animal Crossing: New Horizons, was released on March 20th, 2020. I started this game series with Animal Crossing: New Leaf (2005), and I've been hooked since.

When I first played Animal Crossing: New Leaf, I immediately became enthralled with all of the cute villagers and ability to manipulate the town into any image I saw fit. Animal Crossing: New Horizons is all that and much more. While multiplayer has always been available in this game series, New Horizons makes it even easier to connect with friends via online play.

Since CoVid-19 has made going outside and hanging out with friends pretty difficult, I have been hosting "parties" in my town on Animal Crossing: New Horizons. I invite a few of my closest friends to a zoom call and we all dress in the weeks theme and sip some adult beverages.

While I usually involve myself with just a theme for people to dress their characters in, I had an idea one night to do a scavenger hunt.
