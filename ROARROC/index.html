ABOUT ROAR

ROAR was born from the void that was created after the closing of the biggest LGBTQ+ oriented/18+ club in Rochester. Our #1 focus is creating a unique safe space for the LGBTQ+ and everyone in the community.

We feature local talent from Rochester & Western NY. Live bands, drag shows including kings and queens, country line dance and free lessons, happiest hours, after work socials, brunch and dinner shows, down to huge party club nights. Our goal is to create a unique safe space for all.

Truely ~ "Something for Everyone"
